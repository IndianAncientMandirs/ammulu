Interesting facts about Meenakshi Amma

  • Meenakshi is a form of Parvati, goddess of strength, grace, and compassion.
  • Meenakshi's marriage to Lord Sundareswarar symbolizes divine unity.
  • The temple has 14 magnificent gopurams with intricate sculptures.
  • The West Tower of the temple inspired Tamil Nadu's state emblem.
  • The temple's inner sanctum is dedicated to Lord Sundareswarar (Shiva).
