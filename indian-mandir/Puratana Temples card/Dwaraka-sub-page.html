Dwaraka

Dwarka, one of the most significant cities in Indian history and mythology, is considered the first capital of Gujarat and is deeply associated with Lord Krishna. Dwarka had a population of 38,873 in 2011, and its main festival, Janmashtami, is celebrated in the month of Bhadrapada. The name "Dwarka" means "gateway" and is also referred to as "Mokshapuri," "Dwarkamati," and "Dwarkavati.
